## Scope

This report checks whether `remove-ai-flavor` covers the common de-AI-writing cases that motivated the skill:

- `不是...而是`
- `先...再`
- `真正...的是`
- route-marker phrases
- fake engagement questions at the end

The public fixtures are synthetic and safe to publish. Two local private samples were also tested, but their full text is not included in this repository.
